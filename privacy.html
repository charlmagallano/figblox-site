Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

Figblox is a two-plugin system for moving UI from Figma into Roblox Studio. This page explains what information Figblox may receive, how it is used, and which third-party services are involved.

What Figblox does not do

Figblox does not run its own backend for uploading your Figma files, exported JSON, images, or Roblox project data. Your design data stays on your side unless you choose to share it yourself.

Purchases and licenses

If you purchase Figblox, payment, billing, and license processing may be handled by a third-party provider such as Polar. That provider may collect information such as your name, email address, billing details, and purchase or license status. Figblox may receive limited purchase or license details from that provider so the product can be delivered, licenses verified, and support handled.

Figma plugin

The Figma plugin may store license-related data locally in Figma storage (for example a license key and cache timestamps) so you do not have to enter it every session. Exporting designs to JSON and images happens in Figma on your device. Figblox does not receive that content on its own servers.

Roblox Studio plugin

The Roblox Studio plugin may store license-related data locally in Studio plugin settings on your computer. It may connect to a license provider to verify your key. It does not upload your place contents, imported JSON, or project data to a Figblox server.

Website

If you use the Figblox website, the hosting or CDN platforms that serve the site may collect basic technical information (such as logs or usage signals). Figblox does not operate a separate analytics product on top of that unless stated otherwise here.

Discord and support

If you join the Figblox Discord server or contact us for support, we may keep the messages you send so we can answer questions and resolve product issues. Discord processes your activity under its own privacy policy.

How information is used

Figblox uses the limited information described above to run the product, verify licenses, respond to support, process purchases where applicable, and meet legal obligations.

Third-party services

Figblox may rely on services operated by others, including:

  • Polar, for purchases, billing, and license verification
  • Figma, for running the Figma plugin
  • Roblox, for running the Studio plugin
  • Discord, if you join the community server

Please refer to those providers for their privacy practices.

Data retention

Information Figblox directly receives (for example support messages) is kept only as long as needed for support, legal, and accounting purposes. License data stored inside the plugins stays on your device until you remove it or uninstall the plugins.

Children

Figblox is not intended for children under 13, and Figblox does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of information that Figblox directly holds. Use the contact options below to send a privacy request.

Changes

This policy may be updated over time. When it changes, the latest version will appear on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

Email urleobf@gmail.com for general questions, privacy requests, or licensing. For purchase-specific issues you can also use the contact options on our checkout page. If you reached out through Discord or another channel, we will respond there when possible.
