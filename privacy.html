Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Introduction

Figblox (“Figblox,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) provides software plugins for Figma and Roblox Studio and operates this website. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information in connection with the Figblox plugins, related documentation, and marketing pages (collectively, the “Services”).

Information We Collect

Information you provide. If you contact us (for example, via email or a support form), we collect the content of your message and associated contact details you choose to share.

Plugin and editor context. The Figblox Figma plugin and Roblox Studio plugin run inside Figma and Roblox environments. Processing required to export or import UI data may occur locally on your machine or within those platforms as part of normal plugin execution. We design Figblox so that routine design-to-import workflows do not require uploading your full project files to Figblox-controlled servers.

Website technical data. Like many websites, our hosting provider may log basic technical information when you visit this site, such as IP address, browser type, referring URLs, and timestamps. That information is used for security, diagnostics, and aggregated statistics.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services;
  • Respond to support requests and communicate about updates or security notices;
  • Monitor abuse, protect integrity, and comply with legal obligations;
  • Analyze aggregated or de-identified usage to improve documentation and product quality.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that assist us (for example, hosting, email delivery, or analytics infrastructure), bound by confidentiality and processing agreements where applicable. We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or security of Figblox, our users, or the public.

Third-Party Platforms

Figblox integrates with Figma and Roblox. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective terms and privacy policies. We encourage you to review Figma and Roblox privacy notices to understand how those services handle account and usage data.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Support correspondence may be retained to resolve ongoing issues and maintain internal records of communications.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to protect information in our control. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

International Users

If you access the Services from outside your home country, your information may be processed in countries where we or our providers operate. Those countries may have data protection laws that differ from those where you live.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain personal information, or to object to particular processing activities. To exercise applicable rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your request before responding.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will post the revised policy on this page and update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the Services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy, except where prohibited by law.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact Figblox at the support email published on this website or your purchase receipt. Replace this paragraph with your official contact channel before publishing.
