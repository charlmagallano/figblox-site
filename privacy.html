Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Overview

Figblox provides Figma and Roblox Studio plugins plus this website. This policy describes what information may be processed when you use those services.

Data we collect

When you use the plugins, design data you export stays under your control: it moves between Figma and your Roblox Studio session according to your actions. We may collect basic usage or account information if you log in or purchase a license, such as email and transaction records, as needed to provide the service.

Cookies and analytics

This static site may use minimal analytics or hosting logs from your browser or CDN provider. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact options on our checkout page.
